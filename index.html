Cecilia Y. Sui

PhD Candidate at Washington University St. Louis

Hi! I am a PhD Candidate in the Department of Political Science at Washington University in St. Louis advised by Prof. Jacob Montgomery and Christopher Lucas. My broad research area is in computational social science. In particular, I am interested in how to build and customize computational models to address measurement problems in political science across different modalities. Substantively, I like to study why political campaigns are persuasive among different target groups.
